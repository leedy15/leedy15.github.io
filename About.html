About Me

Hello, I'm Dylan

 

I like JavaScript and everything web. Creating something for people to use and enjoy is a passion of mine. It started when I was younger and made Html sites for Myspace for lunch money in middle and high school, and then turned me into a chef. I rediscovered my passion for programming and now I'm here to make the web a beautiful creative place.

Since starting my career as a software developer I have taught elementary school students basic programming logic in an after school program, worked as an intern at The Tech Academy after graduation and moved up into an instructor position and have done contracting work for Prosper IT Consulting. I'm currently looking for my next big place in this industry to make an impact.

I built this site with the techniques that I learned from previous projects that I have been apart of. The tricky part was getting the JavaScript on the background to work properly for desktop and mobile versions. However, even with the struggles I faced I put everything I had into this site and I had a lot of fun along the way.
